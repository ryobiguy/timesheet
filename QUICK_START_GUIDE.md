# Clockly Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Clockly! This guide will help you get started in 5 minutes.

## üöÄ For Company Administrators

### Step 1: Create Your Company Account

1. Go to [your-domain.com/signup](your-domain.com/signup)
2. Enter your company name and email
3. You'll receive a **6-digit company code** - **save this!** You'll need it to add workers.

### Step 2: Add Your First Jobsite

1. Log in to your dashboard
2. Click **"Jobsites"** in the sidebar
3. Click **"Add Jobsite"**
4. Enter:
   - Jobsite name (e.g., "123 Main St Construction")
   - Address (or click map to set location)
   - Geofence radius (default: 100 meters)
5. Click **"Save"**

### Step 3: Add Workers

**Option A: Workers Self-Register (Recommended)**
1. Share your **6-digit company code** with workers
2. Workers download the Clockly app
3. They register using: email, name, password, and your company code

**Option B: Admin Creates Workers**
1. Go to **"Users"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add User"**
3. Enter worker details
4. Worker receives email with login instructions

### Step 4: Assign Workers to Jobsites

1. Go to **"Assignments"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Assignment"**
3. Select:
   - Worker
   - Jobsite
   - Start date (optional)
   - End date (optional)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Step 5: Workers Clock In/Out

Workers use the mobile app:
1. Open Clockly app
2. See assigned jobsites
3. Tap **"Clock In"** when arriving at jobsite
4. Tap **"Clock Out"** when leaving
5. GPS automatically verifies location

### Step 6: Approve Time Entries

1. Go to **"Approvals"** in the sidebar
2. Review pending time entries
3. Click **"Approve"** or **"Dispute"** for each entry
4. Use **"Select All"** for bulk approval

### Step 1: Download the App

- **iOS:** Download from App Store (coming soon)
- **Android:** Download from Google Play (coming soon)

### Step 2: Register

1. Open the Clockly app
2. Tap **"Register"**
3. Enter:
   - Your email
   - Your name

   - **Company code** (get this from your supervisor)
4. Tap **"Sign Up"**

### Step 3: Clock In/Out

1. Open the app
2. You'll see your assigned jobsites
3. When you arrive at a jobsite:
   - Tap **"Clock In"**
   - App verifies your GPS location
4. When you leave:
   - Tap **"Clock Out"**
   - Your time entry is automatically created

### Step 4: Check Your Time

- View your time entries in the app
- See which entries are pending approval
- View approved entries

## üîß Troubleshooting

### "Invalid company code" error
- Double-check the 6-digit code
- Contact your administrator for the correct code

### GPS not working
- Ensure location permissions are enabled
- Check that you're within the jobsite geofence radius
- Try refreshing the app

### Can't clock in
- Make sure you're assigned to the jobsite
- Check that you're within the geofence radius (shown on map)
- Ensure GPS is enabled on your device

### Time entry not showing
- Time entries appear after clocking out
- Check with your supervisor if it needs approval
- Refresh the approvals page
